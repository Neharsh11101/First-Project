GULAB JAMUN

Gulab jamun is a popular Indian dessert of fried dough balls soaked in a rose-scented syrup. The name literally translates to "rose water berry" or "rose berry".




*Ingredients
  • Milk Solids
  • Flour
  • Cardamom
  • Rose Water
  • Saffron
  • Garnished with Almonds
  • Other spices
